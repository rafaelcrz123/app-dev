# *The Flash* (TV Series)
The Flash is an americcan superhero television series and it is based on the DC comics character **Barry Allen**.

## Plot Summary
After being struck by lightning and doused in chemicals, CSI investigator Barry Allen awakens from a nine-month coma with the power of super speed. With his new powers, he becomes "The Flash" and fights crime in Central City.

### Key Characters
* **Barry Allen** (The Flash)
* **Iris West-Allen** (Journalist)
* **Caitlin Snow** (Killer Frost)
*  **Cisco Ramon** (Vibe)
*  **Joe West** (Police Detective)
